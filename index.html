about

Professionally focused on UX and UI Design and Photography working on the Westside of Los Angeles.


I am an experienced design professional who can present efficient and creative solutions to meet user’s interaction and business needs. My technical knowledge and creative vision are key to taking the design process from understanding the problem, generating possible solutions, analyzing, prototyping, and assessing solutions to direct the behavior of Users the endpoint efficiently. I feel these are very important aspects to any creative and bring them to each and every project I work on.
